Essential Ford Mustang Maintenance Tips

March 25, 2025

Keep your Ford Mustang in peak condition with essential maintenance practices. Regular oil changes using synthetic oil are crucial, along with proper cooling system care to prevent overheating. Transmission maintenance varies between manual and automatic systems, while brake inspections should occur every 5,000 miles. Don't neglect suspension alignment and electrical system checks. Follow a detailed preventive maintenance schedule, from routine oil changes to major 60,000-mile services. For modified Mustangs, consider increased maintenance requirements. Using Ford-recommended parts, keeping detailed records, and addressing issues promptly will ensure your Mustang's longevity and optimal performance.
