About Us

Founded in 2005 by a team of passionate Mustang enthusiasts and certified mechanics, we've dedicated ourselves to creating the most comprehensive and user-friendly workshop manuals for Ford Mustang owners. Our journey began in a small garage in Detroit, where we recognized the need for more detailed, accessible repair information.

Our mission is to empower Mustang owners with the knowledge and confidence to maintain and repair their vehicles. We believe in the importance of detailed, accurate information and continuous learning. Our core values include technical excellence, customer education, and preserving the legacy of America's favorite pony car.
